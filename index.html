Andrew Johnson was the 17th President of the United States, leading the nation through the turbulent Reconstruction era. His presidency was marked by deep political conflicts, impeachment, and significant national change.

Did You Know?

Andrew Johnson was the first U.S. president to be impeached, but he survived removal by just one vote.

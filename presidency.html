Major Accomplishments

  • Oversaw the passage of the 13th Amendment, which abolished slavery.
  • Facilitated the purchase of Alaska in 1867.

Challenges & Controversies

  • Clashed with Congress over Reconstruction policies.
  • Became the first U.S. president to be impeached, though he was acquitted.

Early Life

Born on December 29, 1808, in Raleigh, North Carolina, Andrew Johnson came from a poor family. His father died young, leaving the family in financial hardship.

Education

Johnson had little formal schooling and was largely self-taught, learning to read and write with the help of his mother.

Early Career

Before entering politics, Johnson worked as a tailor in Tennessee. His rise in local prominence led him into politics.
